# Programming AVR Devices

## Find a BluePill

Download [FASTUSBasp](https://github.com/amitesh-singh/FASTUSBasp) from GitHub
and flash to STM32 Blue Pill

## AVR ISP Pinout
Function | Pin      | Pin   | Function
-----    |:-----:   |:-----:| -----
MISO     |    1     |   2   | VCC
SCK      |    3     |   4   | MOSI
NRST     |    5     |   6   | GND
